### Tips!

- Powered by excellent <a href="https://github.com/rajgoel/reveal.js-plugins/tree/master/chart" target="_blank">Charts Plugin</a>
- Simply enable `charts` property in your `PITCHME.yaml`
- Press `F` to go fullscreen!
